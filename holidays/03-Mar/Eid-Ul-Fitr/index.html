EID AL FITR

MARCH 20

Eid al-Fitr is a Muslim festival that marks the end of Ramadan, the month of fasting. It is celebrated after the sighting of the new moon and is known as the "Festival of Breaking the Fast." On this day, Muslims perform a special prayer, give charity to the poor, wear new or clean clothes, and share meals with family and friends. It is a joyful time of gratitude, forgiveness, and unity in the community.
