BLACK SATURDAY

Black Saturday is the day between Good Friday and Easter Sunday in the Christian tradition, observed as a time of mourning and reflection. It commemorates the period when Jesus Christ lay in the tomb after his crucifixion, symbolizing a day of silence, grief, and waiting.
