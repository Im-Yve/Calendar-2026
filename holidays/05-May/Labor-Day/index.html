LABOR DAY

MAY 1

Labor Day is a holiday celebrated in many countries to honor the contributions and achievements of workers and the labor movement. In the Philippines, it is observed on May 1 and is known as Araw ng Manggagawa. The day recognizes the hard work, rights, and dignity of workers across all sectors.
